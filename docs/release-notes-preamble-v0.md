> **Experimental developer preview.** All v0.x releases are
> experimental (D33): the `napflow/v1` flow schema, run-history and
> event formats, HTTP API, and UI may change between v0.x releases —
> breaking changes are called out in release notes, and no
> compatibility is guaranteed before v1.0. napflow assumes **trusted
> workspaces** (flows and `nodes.py` execute real Python on your
> machine) and is **localhost-only** — do not expose `napf ui` beyond
> loopback. Known limits and the hardening plan:
> [docs/PLAN.md](https://github.com/nickosh/napflow/blob/main/docs/PLAN.md).
